About the Author & Beyond by BAO

Hi, I'm Bao!

About two years ago, I discovered AI generative art. As a former professional photographer, I cannot stand visual compromise, so my main passion is flawless photorealism. It's incredible to see how, in a short time, technology has advanced from timid experiments to results indistinguishable from reality. Today, neural networks have reached a level where the line between the real and the generated is blurred — and this philosophical uncertainty is the core of my work.

Having started with creating static artistic images, with the advent of video models, I plunged into vivifications — the process of bringing my art and real photographs to life. I also enjoy bringing back to life old, time-damaged, and black-and-white shots.

But the main focus of my work lately is the Beyond by BAO project. This is an exploration of the space beyond the frame. For me, the original shot is just a keyhole. We see only a fragment, but what is hiding to the left? Where does the hero's gaze go? What will happen to him a minute later? I preserve the essence of the original, but create a whole alternative universe around it, previously unseen.

Technology allows me to stitch documentary reality and fiction together so that the seam becomes invisible. Fact and fantasy merge into one. I create beauty in all its forms — from visual art to poetry — capturing the fragility of the moment by any means available.

All my artistic experiments exist on pure enthusiasm and require considerable personal resources. I sincerely ache for beauty, and your likes and warm comments always remain my main reward. However, if my work is especially close to you, you can support the project financially via the donate link below.

I am also open for private commissions: I can expand the boundaries of reality and create a unique story from any photograph dear to your heart, following your own script.
