About the Author & Beyond by BB Art

Hi, I'm Bao!

About two years ago, I got heavily into neural networks. As a former pro photographer, I can't stand compromises in quality, so my main obsession is flawless photorealism. It's funny: just yesterday AI was churning out plastic six-fingered mutants, and today even I, with a trained eye, often can't distinguish some generations from real photos. The line between the real and the generated is thinning every day – and I don't know about you, but this process wildly motivates and hooks me, resonating with my philosophical ideas.

At first, I just generated beautiful photos, but when neural networks learned to produce good video quality, I got hooked on vivifications – bringing my best works, others' works, and real photographs to life. I also love restoring retro photography classics (old, time-damaged shots).

But my main focus right now is the reality expansion project, Beyond by BB Art. This is essentially stepping beyond the visible frame. For me, the original shot is just a keyhole. We see only a fragment captured by the lens, but what is there on the right? On the left? Where is the hero looking? What happens a minute later? I take the essence of the original, preserving the appearance and atmosphere of the shot, and build an entire alternative universe around it, limited only by my imagination :)

Technology lets me stitch fact and fiction together so tightly that the seam disappears. You no longer know where reality ends and my fantasy begins. And this is truly impressive and amazing.

I try to capture beauty in all its forms and manifestations – from visuals (photos, videos, art) to literary stories and poetry. But my main passion remains photography and cinematography. My dream is to one day shoot a feature film that will be hard to distinguish from reality, and I am gradually moving towards this dream.

All this magic runs on bare enthusiasm and drains my time and significant financial resources. I sincerely ache for beauty, and your likes and warm comments are my main reward. But if my work resonates with you, you can support the project financially via the donate link below.

I'm also open for private commissions: I can expand the boundaries of reality and create a unique story from any photograph dear to your heart, following your or my script.
